The Greatness of Nelson Mandela

Mandela was President of South Africa from 1994-99.

Important details in Mandela's life

  • Born July 18, 1918 in the South African village of Mvezo
  • 1939 Attended Univeristy of Fort Hare
  • Established African National Congress Youth League in 1944
  • Led 1953 campaign for the Defiance of Unjust Laws
  • In 1962 the Apartheid Government banned the ANC
  • Arrested in 1962 for treason and sent to Robben Island Prison
  • Released from prison in 1990 by FW DeKlerk
  • Elected President of South Africa in 1994
  • United Nations declared July 18 "Nelson Mandela Day"
  • Died on December 5, 2013 from a lung infection
